Delay bot-generated input before authoritative processing by the arithmetic mean of current connected human peers' one-way latency, rounded up to whole simulation ticks. Exclude bots and disconnected peers, use zero added delay in bot-only matches, recompute the mean from current samples for newly generated commands, and preserve each already-queued command's scheduled processing tick. Expose deterministic tests and diagnostics for the sampled average and effective bot delay.
