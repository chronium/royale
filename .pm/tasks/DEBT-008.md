Preserve the reusable Blender export and asset pipeline where useful, but do not rely on Blender MCP procedural scene generation as the primary design workflow. Either redesign the existing arena or replace it with a human-authored map that passes owner validation for layout, visual quality, clipping, collision alignment, sightlines, traversal, stairs, interior readability, and iteration cost. Until then, treat courtyard-compound as an experimental pipeline and automated-validation fixture rather than a production-quality gameplay arena.
