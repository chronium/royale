## Overview

The project overview wiki is split into focused pages so product scope, technology choices, and MVP criteria can evolve independently.

Start here:

* `project-overview/overview` - full overview, goals, stack, scope, principles, and MVP definition
* `project-overview/goals` - cross-platform, server-authoritative, focused-engine, and vertical-slice goals
* `project-overview/technology-stack` - .NET, SDL3, SDL GPU, Box3D, and ImGui roles
* `project-overview/game-scope` - initial gameplay scope and first-version non-goals
* `project-overview/development-principles` - development principles and MVP success criteria

The key premise remains: a small custom .NET stack should prove a complete cross-platform, server-authoritative battle-royale game loop.
