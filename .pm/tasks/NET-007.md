Immediately simulate local inputs on the client before receiving server confirmation.

## Acceptance Notes

- `NetworkClientRuntime` now owns v1 local movement prediction for accepted clients.
- Prediction loads collision from the accepted `ServerAccept.MapId` through an injectable map loader, defaulting to `MapCatalog.LoadById`; if the map/collision world cannot be created, the client falls back to authoritative snapshot presentation.
- Prediction seeds from the first authoritative local-player snapshot, stores successfully sent commands in a bounded pending-input buffer, and immediately steps local movement with `PlayerMovementIntent` plus `KinematicCharacterController` against `MapStaticCollisionWorld`.
- `ClientNetworkState.LatestSnapshot` remains authoritative-only. Presentation builds a copied snapshot with only the local predicted player substituted for camera/debug rendering when prediction is available; remote players remain snapshot-driven.
- Incoming snapshot acknowledgements drop pending inputs. When no pending inputs remain, prediction resyncs to the authoritative local snapshot. Correction/replay, interpolation, and bad-network policy remain deferred.
- Updated `architecture/networking` to document implemented v1 prediction and remaining deferred networking work.
