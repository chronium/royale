Fixed-facing world text billboards can still jump or move abruptly when the camera looks at, or nearly parallel to, the authored text plane around 90-degree viewing angles. Pink Blurg glyph-edge artifacts were fixed under RENDER-009, so this bug should focus on stabilizing fixed-facing billboard projection/culling behavior without changing the accepted atlas-edge fix.

Observed during human validation of RENDER-009 on 2026-07-06. Repro: launch the client, inspect the fixed-facing smoke label, and move the camera/view until the label plane is close to edge-on. The label can visibly jolt across the screen instead of fading, smoothly culling, or remaining stable.

Acceptance notes:
- Fixed-facing labels should not jump or streak across the screen near edge-on angles.
- Decide whether edge-on behavior should cull, fade, clamp, or use a different stable projection model before implementation.
- Preserve camera-facing label behavior and Blurg BGRA/transparent-white atlas handling from RENDER-009.
- Add or update projection tests for the chosen edge-on behavior.
