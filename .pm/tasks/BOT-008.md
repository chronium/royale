Allow bots to orient, aim, and fire the authoritative rifle through normal input commands while respecting equipped state, ammunition, fire cadence, health, death, and match-phase combat gating. Bots must never track targets or fire hitscan with perfect accuracy: generate a non-zero, configurable, deterministically seeded angular aim error plus explicit reaction and aim-settling delay in bot look intent before it reaches the unchanged authoritative weapon and raycast path. Error must evolve coherently rather than rerolling as independent per-tick noise, remain bounded and testable, and support future difficulty tuning. Confirm concrete tuning values and error model with the project owner before implementation.
